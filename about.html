About StarTech

StarTech is a technology company founded by Sammy Lusasi, also known as Starboy. With a passion for technology and innovation, Starboy has built StarTech to provide training in computer courses and programming for students and businesses.

Sammy Lusasi is currently pursuing ICT Technician Level 6 at Kenya Coast National Polytechnic, where he is gaining advanced knowledge in ICT. He specializes in Java programming, Android Studio development, and database management.

Through StarTech, Sammy aims to empower individuals by equipping them with the technical skills required to thrive in the modern digital world.
